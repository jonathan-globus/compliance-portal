Welcome to the Globus Compliance Portal. This site provides an overview of Globus security practices, High Assurance capabilities, and resources to support institutional compliance, privacy, and data stewardship requirements.

---

## Security Overview

Globus is designed to support secure, reliable research data management across institutions. The platform incorporates strong authentication, encrypted data transfer, granular access controls, and detailed audit logging. These capabilities help institutions meet internal policy requirements and external regulatory obligations.

For additional information, see the Globus Security Overview:

[https://www.globus.org/security](https://www.globus.org/security)

---

## High Assurance

High Assurance collections provide enhanced security controls for sensitive or regulated data. These controls include strong authentication, restricted session lifetimes, mandatory encryption, and expanded audit logging. Institutions configure and manage these collections to align with their compliance requirements.

Learn more about High Assurance:

[https://www.globus.org/high-assurance](https://www.globus.org/high-assurance)

---

## Sensitive Data Handling

Globus supports secure workflows for sensitive and regulated data, including data subject to institutional policies or external requirements such as HIPAA, NIH, or other research data protections. High Assurance collections and related controls help institutions meet their compliance obligations while enabling researchers to work efficiently.

For more information, see the dedicated page on Sensitive Data Handling:

[Sensitive Data Handling](./sensitive-data-handling)
